Instructions

Before you start playing the game, you'll receive training. In each training round, a red line will appear for 1 second and then disappear.

​

Two seconds later, you will be asked to recreate the line as accurately as possible.

Move the mouse left and right to adjust the size of the blue bar, and then click to enter your response. You will have 2 seconds to respond.

​

During training, a gray bar will appear above your guess for two seconds to show you the correct answer.

​

You will only be shown this bar during the training phase of the game.
