Instructions

Once you've finished the training rounds, you will play the game with a partner who has also finished training. Again, your goal is to submit the most accurate guess possible.

At the beginning of each round, you and your partner will be shown a red line. It will appear for 1 second and then disappear.

​

After waiting for 2 seconds, you will have 2 seconds to make your first guess individually. Again, move the mouse left and right to adjust the size of the blue bar, and then click to enter your response.

After you and your partner have submitted your first guess, you will see your guess (in blue) above your partner's guess (in green).

​
​

You and your partner will not be able to see the correct answer, but each of you can decide whether to accept your guess or submit a new guess. If either of you submits a new guess, you will both be asked to decide again to accept your guess or submit a new one.

You and your partner will repeat this until you both decide not to make any additional changes and submit your final guesses.

After you finish each round, you and your partner will move onto the next round.
